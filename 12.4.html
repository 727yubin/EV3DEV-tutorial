LEDs

Your brick has 4 LEDs. There are 2 red and 2 green LEDs on the left and right.

To turn on the LEDs, do ev3.Leds.set_color(ev3.Leds.LEFT, ev3.Leds.RED)

The possible positions are ev3.Leds.LEFT ev3.Leds.RIGHT

The possible colors are ev3.Leds.RED ev3.Leds.AMBER ev3.Leds.GREEN ev3.Leds.ORANGE ev3.Leds.YELLOW.

To turn off the LEDs, do ev3.Leds.all_off()

To make your life simpler, do from ev3.Leds import * to avoid typing ev3.Leds every single time.
