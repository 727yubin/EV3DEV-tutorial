print()

Now, let’s do something more than arithmetic. Type print(“Hello, World!”) and press Enter.

>>> print(“Hello, World!”) Hello, World!

And voila, your first program. What print does is write to the screen, NOT print to a printer.

Tasks:

  1. Make the computer write “Hello, “ and your name on the screen. For example, I would have to make the computer write “Hello, Yubin.”
  2. Make the computer print the following: Hello. I am Python 3.

    Make sure they come out on two separate lines.
